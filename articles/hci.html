    Human Cat Interaction?

    Although I wish this were an article about cats, it's really about Human Computer Intercation. I don't imagine that this acronym is well known outside of academia, but it should be. snap snap!

    Why you may ask? Well, before I answer your question, let's examine what you are currently doing. You are looking at my website, using a mouse to maybe swtich to a tab with more interesting content like cat videos, enjoying the ability to have four different overlapping window screens simulataneously on view because you are so good at multi-tasking...Well my friend, you have HCI research to thank for that.
